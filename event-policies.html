Event Policies

The practical rules and expectations that help the weekend run smoothly.

Safety

Helmets are required while riding. Ride within your limits, follow instructor guidance, and alert staff if something feels unsafe.

Camping

Camping is part of the festival experience. Keep bikes and belongings secure, respect quiet hours, and follow all park guidance.

Registration

Ticket tiers help make the event more accessible. Clinic add-ons, demo bike requests, dietary notes, and accessibility needs can be handled during registration.

Weather

Outdoor programming may shift because of trail conditions, storms, heat, or safety guidance. Bring layers and rain protection.

Community Care

Harassment, discrimination, threats, or unsafe behavior are not welcome. Staff may remove participants who violate community expectations.

Photos

Ask before taking or sharing identifiable photos of other participants. Respect people who do not want to be photographed.
