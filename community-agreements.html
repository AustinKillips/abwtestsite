Community Agreements

Shared expectations for care, consent, learning, repair, and safer participation.

How We Gather

Make space. Ask first. Take care of each other.

Practice Consent

Ask before touching people, bikes, gear, bodies, or personal space. Respect no, respect maybe, and respect changed minds.

Assume Growth

We are all learning. Stay accountable, listen with care, and make repair when harm happens.

Center Belonging

Honor names, pronouns, identities, bodies, skill levels, and lived experiences. No one should have to prove they belong.

Share The Trail

Ride within your limits, communicate clearly, support new riders, and help keep the weekend low-pressure and joyful.
