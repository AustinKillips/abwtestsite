Grit MTB Festival

Event Info

September 25-27, 2026 at Centennial Park in Fayetteville, Arkansas.

The Basics

A full weekend of riding, camping, meals, workshops, and community.

Use this as the top-level scan before visitors open the details below.

Ticket Information

Use tiered ticket pricing to meet people where they are: subsidized, median, unsubsidized, and single-day passes. Skills clinics can be added during registration, and the First Touch clinic is free for MTB-curious riders.

Bikes

Demo bikes are available for riders who do not have access to a mountain bike. Local rentals, bike shipping details, and storage reminders can live here as the event details firm up.

Camping, Travel, Meals

Camping is included with festival tickets. The festival takes over Centennial Park for the weekend, with dispersed camping, nearby hotels, meals, morning coffee and tea, sparkling water, and refill stations.

Activities
  • Morning yoga
  • Workshops
  • Gear exchange
  • Group rides
  • Keynote speaker
  • Raffle, giveaways, and dance party
Weekend Timeline

Thursday

Setup, early arrivals, camping load-in, and space-making for the weekend.

Friday

Check-in, meals, clinics, group rides, workshops, happy hour, dinner, and evening programming.

Saturday

More clinics, rides, workshops, community time, shared meals, and evening gathering.

Sunday

Breakfast, guided rides, workshops, yoga, goodbyes, cleanup, and roll-out.
